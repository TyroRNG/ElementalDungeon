Magic based system
12 elementen

--Light side--

 - Fire
 - Water
 - Nature
 - Light
 - Wind
 - Earth

--Dark side--

 - Electric
 - Ice
 - Poison
 - Darkness
 - Psychic
 - Metal

Levels of restrictions 

High------------------ 
Element Purist -- Focus on a specific element in both sides


Medium----------------
Light purist -- Focus on all light elements
Dark Purist -- Focus on all dark elements


Low-------------------
All rounder -- Jack of all traids master of none




Items are non scaling but instead set
Items will have the same level of effect no matter the your current scaling
